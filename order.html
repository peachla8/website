F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where Do You Deliver?

    We primarily deliver to the general Central Texas region encompassing San Antonio and Austin.

  • Can I order same day delivery?

    Sometimes peaches can be unpredictable and there may be dry periods between varieties, as well as the harvesting days. It is best to place an order a week or two in advance to guarantee delivery close to your desired date. Also allow for a few days for the peaches to ripen if you are planning to use them for an event. Contact us for special delivery requests.

  • Will You Deliver To Other Texas Cities?

    For orders over 25 bushels, we will deliver to any city in Texas. If you would like to organize a group order and have us bring Fredericksburg peaches to your city, contact us and we will facilitate your request.
